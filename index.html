Programming Projects

  • Treasure Hunt

    This is a dungeon-crawler game where you have to fight enemies and find clues to get to the Treasure. You must first go find the key to open the locked door in the Main room. Once you unlock this door you will find a mysterious piece of paper. Find the Treasure and use this paper to win! All of the local scores will be kept in the local leaderboard! Change your name in the settings and challenge your friends! This was developed by Zachary Current, Brayden Ruch, Kathryn Rohlfing, and Evan Williams.

  • Carbon Counter

    This is an app for android. It was built in android studio, utilizing Volley for the front-end and springboot for the back-end. The purpose of this app is to allow the user to track carbon related stats, inform the user on their carbon output, and suggest tips on how they can reduce their carbon footprint. These tips can be recommended based on their stats. This app was developed Zachary Current, Colton Glick, Andrew Pester, and Morgan Funk.
